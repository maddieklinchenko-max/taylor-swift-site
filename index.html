A transitional record blending country and pop elements; notable for its emotional range and wide commercial success. Also re-recorded as Red (Taylor’s Version).

About

Taylor Swift is a singer-songwriter who rose from country music beginnings to become a global pop icon. Born in 1989, she started performing as a child, moved to Nashville as a teenager, and released her self-titled debut album in her mid-teens.

Known for her storytelling songwriting, Taylor has explored multiple musical styles — country, pop, indie/folk, and alternative — across a series of acclaimed albums. She writes or co-writes much of her material and is recognized for honest, personal lyrics and memorable melodies.

Over her career she has received many awards, including multiple Grammy Awards, and built a dedicated global fan community. Taylor is also active in philanthropy, supports artists' rights, and often engages with fans through social media and surprise releases.

This fan site is for informational and community purposes only. For in-depth coverage, official photos, tour dates, and verified news, visit Taylor's official site or her artist pages on major music platforms. Please avoid posting copyrighted lyrics or unlicensed images here.
